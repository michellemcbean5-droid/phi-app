# Google Play Checklist

## App Signing Setup
1. Generate the Google Play service account JSON and save it as `mobile/google-play-key.json`.
2. Create an Android app in Play Console with package name `com.princehaulintelligence.app`.
3. Enable Play App Signing and store the upload key securely.
4. Configure EAS credentials with `eas credentials` before the first production submission.
